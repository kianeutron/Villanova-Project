Villanova University Overview

Villanova University is a prestigious private Catholic research university located in Villanova, Pennsylvania, a suburb of Philadelphia. It was established in 1842 by the Order of Saint Augustine, making it the oldest Catholic university in Pennsylvania.

Rankings

Villanova consistently ranks highly in national university rankings. Villanova offers a vibrant campus life with numerous student organizations, clubs, and activities. The Villanova Wildcats, the university’s athletic teams, compete in NCAA livision I sports, primarily in the Big East Conference. The university’s basketball team, in particular, has a storied history and has won multiple national championships.
